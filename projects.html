Banana Island by Anantara

Anantara Doha Island Resort is an uninhabited offshore island situated approximately 31/2 Kilometers from new Doha International Airport (closest main land point) and 11 Kilometers east of Doha City. The project involves the construction, development and operation of a 5-Star island resort property.The island resort incorporates the use of tubular steel piles that support various structures such as villas or walkways. The steel tubular piles suffered premature severe corrosion a shortly after installation. DUCORR has been retained to propose a durability solution using cathodic protection.
